📋 Our Sacred Baptismal Covenant

When entering into fellowship at Naigobya SDA Church, members declare their faith and alignment with the global body of Christ through these sacred vows and spiritual promises:

1. The Godhead

We accept Jesus Christ as our personal Savior and Lord, believing in God the Father, the Holy Spirit, and the gift of divine grace through the cross.

2. Authority of Scripture

We accept the Holy Scriptures of the Old and New Testaments as the inspired Word of God and the ultimate, sole rule of faith and practice.

3. Cleansing & New Birth

We accept death to old sinful practices, receiving water baptism by full immersion as a public demonstration of washing and new life in Jesus Christ.

4. The Ten Commandments

We declare our intent to look to God's moral law as a reflection of His character, choosing by His grace to live an obedient, holy life.

5. The Holy Seventh-Day Sabbath

We pledge to observe the Seventh-day Sabbath (Saturday) from sunset Friday to sunset Saturday as the memorial of Creation and a sign of our loyalty to God.

6. The Prophetic Church

We acknowledge the Remnant Church of Bible prophecy and accept the Spirit of Prophecy (manifested in the ministry of Ellen G. White) as a historical gift to God's end-time people.

7. Christian Stewardship

We support the local work of the church through regular worship, systematic benevolence (returning a faithful tithe of 10% and giving free-will offerings), and spiritual dedication.

8. Temple of the Holy Spirit

We promise to respect our bodies as holy temples by abstaining from harmful substances, unclean foods, worldly entertainment, and immodest display.

📣 The Three Angels' Messages

Our distinct prophetic identity is anchored in the final proclamation of God's warning and love to a dying world:

The First Angel

"Fear God, and give glory to him; for the hour of his judgment is come..." Calling humanity to return to true worship and honor God as the Creator of heaven and earth.

The Second Angel

"Babylon is fallen, is fallen..." A message warning against spiritual confusion, false systems of worship, and compromised religious structures that fall away from biblical truth.

The Third Angel

"Here is the patience of the saints..." A warning against receiving the mark of the beast, highlighting the final, faithful remnant who keep the commandments of God and have the faith of Jesus.

Our SDA Commitment

As Seventh-day Adventists, we believe in salvation through grace alone, righteousness by faith in Jesus Christ, obedience to God's timeless commandments, and preparation for the soon, literal, audible second coming of Christ in the clouds of heaven. This platform directly supports church ministry, pastoral oversight, membership care, organization, and spiritual unity within the body of Christ.
